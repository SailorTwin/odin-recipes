Ingredients

  • pound ground beef
  • medium onion, chopped
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • small green bell pepper, diced
  • (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • teaspoons dried oregano
  • teaspoons dried basil
  • teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain grease.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
